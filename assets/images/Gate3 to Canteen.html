Route from Gate-3 to Canteen

Canteen: The college canteen is a vibrant, inclusive space designed to serve both students and faculty, offering a welcoming environment where everyone can unwind and enjoy a meal or a quick snack. The canteen provides a variety of food options to cater to different tastes and dietary preferences, with a menu that includes healthy meals, refreshing beverages, regional specialties, and a rotating selection of daily specials.

Spacious seating areas are arranged to accommodate groups of all sizes, with designated sections that allow for quieter dining spaces for faculty while keeping the atmosphere lively and engaging for students. Clean, well-maintained, and affordable, the canteen places a strong emphasis on quality and hygiene.

In addition to its wide menu selection, the canteen offers freshly brewed coffee, tea, and snack options that are perfect for quick breaks.
