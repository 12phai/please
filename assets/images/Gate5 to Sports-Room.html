Route from Gate-5 to Sports-Room

Sports-Room: The Sports Room is a dedicated space on campus designed to support students' physical fitness and sports training. Equipped with a range of indoor sports and fitness equipment, it serves as the perfect place for students to train, practice, or unwind after a long day of academics.

The room is stocked with essential gear for a variety of sports, including table tennis, badminton, and indoor basketball, as well as fitness equipment such as weights, treadmills, and exercise mats. The Sports Room is designed for both individual workouts and group activities, offering an inviting environment where students can focus on building strength, endurance, and coordination.
