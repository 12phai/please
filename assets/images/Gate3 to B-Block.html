Route from Gate-3 to B-BLOCK

B-BLOCK: B-BLOCK is a comprehensive academic and administrative hub, bringing together faculty members from all departments under one roof. This block is structured to optimize accessibility and foster interdisciplinary collaboration:

Ground Floor: Hosts the dedicated faculty offices for the Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) and Allied branches, ensuring close interaction and support for CSE students.

First Floor: Houses the faculty for Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE) and Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE), creating a focused environment for students in these disciplines.

Third Floor: Dedicated to the Aerospace faculty, providing a specialized space for guidance in the field of aviation and aeronautics.

In addition to its faculty facilities, B-BLOCK features a spacious, well-stocked library designed to be a vibrant learning space for all students, with resources across various fields to support academic and research pursuits.

The lower levels serve essential operational functions:

Basement 1: Contains the administrative offices, where students can access essential services and support. Basement 2: Houses cutting-edge labs for EEE and ECE students, providing a hands-on learning environment for practical application of their studies.
