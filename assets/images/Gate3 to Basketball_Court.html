Route from Gate-3 to Basketball-Court

Basketball court: The college basketball court is a hub of energy and excitement, offering students a dedicated space to engage in both casual play and competitive sports. This full-sized, well-maintained court features high-quality flooring that ensures optimal grip, reducing the risk of injury while enhancing playability. Equipped with professional-grade hoops and clear markings for boundaries, the court is ideal for both recreational games and formal college tournaments.

Surrounding bleachers provide seating for spectators, creating an inviting atmosphere for friends, classmates, and fans to cheer on players during games. In addition to fostering teamwork and physical fitness, the court promotes a vibrant sports culture on campus, encouraging students to stay active, build camaraderie, and develop leadership skills through sportsmanship. Whether students are warming up, practicing shots, or competing, the basketball court is a lively space that adds spirit and unity to college life.
