Route from Gate-5 to Girls-Hostel

Girl's Hostel: The Girls' Hostel is a secure, comfortable, and homely space designed to provide a safe and supportive living environment for female students. It offers well-furnished rooms with a choice of single, double, or triple occupancy, each equipped with essential amenities such as comfortable beds, study tables, wardrobes, and ample lighting. The rooms are designed to foster both academic focus and relaxation, ensuring a balanced lifestyle.

Safety is a top priority, with round-the-clock security, CCTV surveillance, and an access control system to ensure the well-being of all residents. Hostel wardens and support staff are present to assist students and ensure smooth day-to-day functioning. The Girls' Hostel also promotes a community-oriented environment, where students can build lasting friendships, engage in activities, and create a sense of belonging during their time at college.
