Route to Playground

Route from Gate-5 to Playground

Playground: The campus playground is a vibrant and spacious area dedicated to sports, physical activities, and recreation. Designed to promote fitness and teamwork, the playground offers a variety of outdoor facilities, including open spaces for team sports like football, cricket, and volleyball, as well as tracks for running and jogging.

The playground is well-maintained with soft, even grass and ample space, making it perfect for informal games, fitness routines, or simply relaxing outdoors. It serves as an active hub for students to engage in sports activities, participate in college tournaments, or enjoy a casual game with friends.
