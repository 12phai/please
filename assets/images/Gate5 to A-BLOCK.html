Route from Gate-5 to A-BLOCK

A-BLOCK: A-BLOCK is a prominent facility designed to foster both learning and hands-on experience for students of the Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) and Allied branches. It boasts three state-of-the-art auditoriums, each equipped with modern amenities and comfortable seating, making it ideal for lectures, seminars, and large-scale events.

On the basement level, A-BLOCK features a series of high-tech, well-equipped laboratories, providing an advanced practical environment for CSE and Allied students to explore cutting-edge technologies, conduct experiments, and engage in project work. These labs are thoughtfully constructed to support both individual and collaborative learning experiences, setting the foundation for innovation and excellence in technical education.
